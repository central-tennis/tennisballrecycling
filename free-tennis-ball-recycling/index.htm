I Want to send in my tennis balls for recycling.

What are the Steps?
  1. Complete the form
  2. There is no longer a minimum quantity of tennis balls to send in (in past was 200+ balls to get a free shipping label), as you are paying for the freight.
  3. We’ll contact you with the approximate weight of the quantity you’re shipping along with our receiving address.
  4. Ship the box to reBounces
  5. reBounces begins the recycling process
Information We Collect in Form
